Detailed Analysis

The task I was most satisfied to complete this week was the responsive redesign of the DocumentList component. My original mockup was awful, without even the decency to wrap across multiple lines:

Luckily, I spotted some clean, appealing markup in a file Fred was working on, and decided that Reactstrap was going to get me out of this mess. I clobbered the entirety of the markup I had built to this point, and rewrote the layout using Reactstrap components.

While the look of the component could still use a little polish, I'm happy to say that the main views of our app are fully responsive, as well as being simple and intuitive to use.
