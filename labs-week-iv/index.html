Detailed Analysis

This week had a unique lack of variety in the tasks I pulled. While I worked on every component across the project, I had the same goal in each file. Most of my effort was spent in the user list of documents, ensuring that it looked good across all breakpoints. Looking at it now, I think our mobile view looks even better than the desktop!

The editor component also received a major overhaul, using bootstrap's layout tricks to place the analysis content above the editor on the small screen. I also corralled the controls into a single group, and made sure they looked consistent across breakpoints.

The last area I concentrated on styling was the new user profile page. I wanted a central column near which the user's profile information could be aligned, which I achieved using fixed-width spans.

I'm very happy with how our site looks and feels now!

Milestone Reflections

The objective this week was completely focused on the front-end, and specifically the user's experience as they navigated the site. In order to be successful this week, we realized that we had to create a uniform experience for the user, where nothing was out of place, and every action available to the user was intuitively obvious. While it may have been the result of pointed prompting, I feel that our team was much more productive in our meetings, where we focused on nailing down choices that would be visible throughout our app, such as the card-based layout.
